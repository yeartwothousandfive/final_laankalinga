1. Terms of Service (Volunteer Agreement)

By registering as a volunteer, you agree to the following terms:

Acceptance of Terms

By creating an account, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to these Terms of Service. If you do not agree, please do not proceed with registration.

Volunteer Conduct

  • Representation: You agree to represent Laan Kalinga professionally and ethically at all times while performing volunteer duties.
  • Commitment: You agree to honor the commitments you make to our events or programs. If you are unable to attend a scheduled shift, you agree to provide reasonable notice to the program coordinator.
  • Safety: You agree to follow all safety guidelines, training instructions, and site-specific rules provided by our staff.
  • No Employment: You acknowledge that your volunteer service is provided on a voluntary basis and does not create an employment relationship between you and Laan Kalinga. You are not entitled to compensation or employee benefits.

Use of Platform

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials. You agree not to use the platform to harass others, distribute spam, or upload harmful content.

Termination

Laan Kalinga reserves the right to terminate your volunteer status or access to this platform at its sole discretion, for reasons including, but not limited to, misconduct, safety concerns, or breach of these terms.

2. Privacy Policy

Laan Kalinga is committed to protecting the privacy of our volunteers.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide during registration, including:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Professional Details: Skills, interests, availability, and emergency contact information.
  • Background Information: If required by specific programs, we may collect information necessary to perform background checks (with your explicit, separate consent).

How We Use Your Information

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Manage volunteer scheduling and communication.
  • Match you with volunteer opportunities that fit your interests and skills.
  • Ensure your safety and coordinate emergency responses if necessary.
  • Comply with legal requirements (such as insurance or local reporting laws).

Data Protection & Disclosure

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We only share your information with authorized staff, project leads, or third-party service providers (e.g., email service providers or volunteer management software) who are strictly bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you.
  • Request the correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request the deletion of your account and personal data (subject to our legal record-keeping obligations).
