About Our Program

The Elders Community Helper System is a web service dedicated to connecting elderly individuals with caregivers through reliable scheduling. Inspired by the Quezon City Office for Senior Citizens Affairs, we aim to provide accessible and efficient care management for our senior community members.

Our platform helps reduce waiting times, manage crowd control, and ensure that senior citizens can access services without the hassle of long queues or uncertainty about service availability.
