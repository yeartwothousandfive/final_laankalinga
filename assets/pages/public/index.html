What Volunteers Help With

Home Visit

A volunteer checks in on the senior at home.

Blood Pressure Check

Basic health monitoring done at the senior's home.

Blood Sugar Check

Volunteers assist with routine blood sugar monitoring.

Medicine Pickup / Delivery

Volunteers fetch medicine from the barangay health center.

Wound Care

Trained volunteers help with basic wound care.

Companion to Clinic

A volunteer accompanies the senior to the health center or hospital.

About Laan-Kalinga

Laan-Kalinga: from the root words "Laan" and "Kalinga", is a community program that organizes volunteer visits for senior citizens.

Family members can also register to help schedule and coordinate visits on behalf of their senior relatives.
