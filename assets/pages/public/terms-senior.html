1. Terms of Service (Senior Participant Agreement)

By registering as a Senior Participant on Laan Kalinga, you (or your authorized representative) agree to the following terms:

Eligibility and Accuracy

  • Information Accuracy: You certify that all personal, health, and contact information provided during registration is accurate and up to date. You agree to update this information should your circumstances change.
  • Account Use: You are responsible for any activity associated with your account, including booking appointments and communicating with staff or volunteers.

Participation and Conduct

  • Respectful Interaction: You agree to treat all Laan Kalinga volunteers, staff, and representatives with respect and courtesy during visits, appointments, and communications.
  • Service Limitations: You understand that Laan Kalinga coordinates volunteer and community services. Unless explicitly stated otherwise, our volunteers are not licensed medical professionals, and the services provided do not replace professional medical or emergency care.
  • Cancellations: If you need to cancel or reschedule a booked visit or service, you agree to provide reasonable advance notice through the platform or by contacting our coordinators.

Health and Safety

For your safety, you agree to disclose any relevant health conditions, allergies, or physical limitations that our volunteers and staff should be aware of when providing assistance or visiting your home.

Termination of Services

Laan Kalinga reserves the right to suspend or terminate your account and access to services if there is a breach of these terms, or if we determine that we can no longer safely or effectively meet your service needs.

2. Privacy Policy (For Senior Participants)

Laan Kalinga is deeply committed to protecting the privacy and dignity of our senior participants.

Information We Collect

To provide safe and effective services, we collect necessary personal information, including:

  • Personal Details: Name, age, birthdate, and home address.
  • Health & Safety Data: General medical history, mobility requirements, dietary restrictions, and specific care needs.
  • Contact Information: Phone numbers, email addresses, and details of designated emergency contacts or family representatives.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Coordinate and schedule appropriate volunteer visits and services.
  • Match you with volunteers or staff who are best suited to your specific needs.
  • Ensure your physical safety and well-being during our programs.
  • Contact your family members or emergency services in the event of an emergency.

Data Protection & Sharing

  • Need-to-Know Basis: Your personal and health-related information is only shared with the specific staff and volunteers assigned to assist you. All personnel are strictly bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • No Third-Party Sales: We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information for commercial purposes.
  • Emergency Disclosure: In the event of a medical emergency, we may share relevant health information with first responders or medical professionals to ensure you receive proper care.

Your Rights

Under applicable data privacy laws, you (or your authorized family representative) have the right to:

  • Access and review the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request updates or corrections to any inaccurate information.
  • Request the deletion of your account and personal data, subject to necessary legal and administrative retention requirements.
