Step-by-Step Appointment Booking

Please fill out all fields marked with an asterisk (*)

This form usually takes 5 10 minutes to complete.

Step 1: What Service Do You Need?

Choose one option that matches your reason for visiting.

Select the health center closest to you or easiest for you to reach.

Step 2: Choose Your Date and Time

Most health centers are open Monday to Friday, 8 AM to 5 PM. Dates that are not available will be greyed out.

Pick a time that fits your schedule. If your preferred time is not available, you can pick a different date or time slot.

If you cannot find an available slot, you may walk in during quiet hours (usually 10 AM 12 PM or 3 PM 5 PM).

Step 3: Your Information

Enter your name or the name of the senior you are booking for.

How old is the person visiting?

If someone else is coming with you (family member, caregiver, etc.), enter their name here. Leave blank if you are coming alone.

Example: +63 912 345 6789 or (02) 1234 5678

A family member or trusted person we can reach if needed.

Choose how you prefer to receive appointment reminders and confirmations.

Step 4: Tell Us More

Briefly describe what you need help with. Example: I need to check my blood pressure because I've been feeling dizzy.

List any medicines you are currently taking, if you know them. This helps the doctor. Example: Amlodipine 5mg daily, Metformin 500mg twice daily.

Tell us if you are allergic to any medicines or substances. Leave blank if no known allergies.

Tell us if you need wheelchair access, transportation help, translation, hearing assistance, or any other support. Leave blank if not needed.

What Happens After You Book?

After Successful Booking

  • You will see a confirmation page with your appointment date, time, and location
  • A list of things to bring will be shown (such as ID, medical records, prescriptions)
  • A confirmation message will be sent to your phone (SMS) or email
  • You can download and save a copy of your booking slip
  • You will receive a reminder message 24 hours before your appointment

If You Need to Cancel or Change

Contact your barangay health center directly. You can find their phone number at the facility or ask during your visit.
